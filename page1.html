First Step: Motherboard + CPU + RAM

Now that we have the pieces and we check that they are compatible with each other, we proceed with the first step to assemble the PC step by step.

To do this, we take the motherboard out of its box and place it on a padded surface so that the components are not damaged (the foam sheet that comes with the board is perfectly fine for us).

The next thing is to remove the processor and place it in the socket. Notice the notch in the corner that has the socket to match the one on the processor. We fit it in such a way that the arrows coincide like this ▷◁ and we press the clamping lever.

The next step is to spread the thermal paste on the processor and adjust the cooling system that we have selected.

Finally we put the RAM memory in its place and we connect the fan connector to the socket on the board, it usually puts CPU FAN and it is the one closest to the processor.



Second Step: Case and power supply

Choosing a good PC case is essential for the optimal functioning of the equipment.

The next step is to condition the tower in order to proceed with the assembly. To do this, the first thing is to place the bolts where we have to fix the plate, add additional components such as readers or fans and adjust the source.

We usually place the source in this step as long as it is easy for us to assemble the motherboard later. If the source is isolated, as in our case, we have no problem, but if not, we should take it into account.

We continue to place elements such as hard drives, internal SSD drive and readers. Once we have all of them in place, we proceed to the distribution of the cables.

BE CAREFUL ⚡

It is important that before introducing the plate we install the metal rear panel that protects its connectors. If we forget we will have to remove it to do it, since it is practically impossible to do it with the plate on without charging anything.

The third step: Introduction of the board and general wiring.

Once everything has been conditioned in the tower and with the fountain prepared, we proceed to put the plate inside.

We will carefully take it by the fan and adjust it until the screw holes coincide with the anchor bolts on the tower.

We use our screwdriver to tighten it and we can proceed to wire the system.

The first connector that we usually put is the ATX power cable for the 24-pin motherboard. Easy to recognize because it is the largest and we only have one.

Later we proceed to connect the power cable of the CPU. It can be 4 or 8 pins. If it is 4, we connect it the same, but we must bear in mind that we may have supply problems if our processor is very powerful. They usually bring it the cheapest and seediest sources.

Finally we go with the hard drives and readers. We will use the power cables from the source to give them power and the SATA cables to connect them to the motherboard.
